When it's cold outside, we all want to relax at home, in warmth and comfort. Electric heaters will make the cold season a little bit warmer and your home more comfortable. Often the cold season comes when no one is waiting for it. The houses are cold, and the central heating has not yet been turned on... In this situation, electric heaters will save you. They can be found in our online store - elektrika.lv.

Electric heater in your home, cottage, garage - this is a simple and fast way of heating, which will provide a comfortable temperature of the air.

Electric heater takes care of your health, because person can get sick, when it’s cold air temperature. Think about it in advance and you will not need to be cold and sick.

Electric heaters are the easiest way of heating, with which you will not have any problems.

There are electric heaters that are made for home use, and there are industrial ones that can be placed, for example, in a warehouse. Both options you can find in our online store. Elektrika cares about the quality of the goods, so we give you a choice of goods from several leading European manufacturers: ABB, Heatcom, Thermor, ENSTO and others.
