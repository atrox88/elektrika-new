Electric meter - is a device that measures the amount of electric energy consumed by a residence, a business, or an electrically powered device.

It happens that the old electric meter no longer meets certain requirements and it needs to be replaced with a new device. This can happen in the following cases:

• the device does not comply with the requirements set by the electrical company;

• the device is damaged;

• moving to a new place of living.

Many electronic power meters in stock in our warehouse, so you can quickly get the product you need.

You can easily find electric meters in our online store. To facilitate the search process, use filters. Thus, you can select the manufacturer, the number of phases and the type of product.

In the category of "electric meters" you can also find pulse and working hours counter.
