Wall lamps can be used as an additional light source, as well as a decorative element in your room. In our online shop you can find wall lamps that perfectly highlight your interior.

Entering the room, we automatically notice particularly interesting interior objects. Such an object can be a designer lamp, which you can find in our store. A simple wall lamp will create a cozy atmosphere not only in your bedroom, but also in other parts of your home. Wall lamp can be plased in the children's room, hallway, in the living room or even in the bathroom!

In Elektrika you will find wall lamps of different styles. Wall lamps in the modern style will inspire you every day, while the classic ones will demonstrate your exquisite taste. The style can be determined by the following elements: lamp shade, material, color, and so on. Many people choose pastel colors, as they not only look beautiful, but also provide comfort in the room. Those who like to take risks can choose the black color that provides the loft style. Everyone can choose what he likes.

When looking for your dream lamp, use filters to make the search process faster and more efficient. For example, you can choose a certain color or power, or something else.

If you have any questions in the process of searching goods, you can write to us and our specialists will help you. We also want to inform you that Elektrika.lv is engaged in both wholesale and retail trade, and also delivers goods not only in Latvia, but also abroad.

About us
